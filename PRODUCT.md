# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
People in Dubai, the UAE, and across the GCC who want confidential online therapy, especially multilingual and multicultural individuals, couples, and families seeking licensed psychologists who understand their language and cultural context.

## Product Purpose
MindGood helps people find and book culturally aware, multilingual therapy that feels clinically trustworthy and emotionally accessible. The product exists to reduce language barriers, cultural friction, and hesitation around getting mental health support online.

## Brand Personality
Calm, trustworthy, culturally aware. The voice should feel reassuring, professional, and emotionally supportive without becoming clinical or overly decorative.

## Anti-references
Avoid generic SaaS-style mental health marketing, overly clinical hospital aesthetics, and loud wellness-app visuals that feel trendy but not trustworthy.

## Design Principles
1. Make trust visible before asking for action.
2. Let cultural familiarity feel natural, not stereotyped.
3. Keep emotional support clear, warm, and easy to act on.
4. Preserve dignity through calm, uncluttered presentation.
5. Prioritize mobile clarity for users reaching out in vulnerable moments.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast and readability standards. Support reduced motion preferences, maintain strong mobile legibility and touch targets, and ensure the experience remains welcoming for multilingual users and people navigating stress, anxiety, or emotional fatigue.
